About Me

I'm Ella Song, a data-driven designer with versatile experience with Fashion, Media, and Technology Companies. Currently, I am completing a Fashion Technology Internship at Blackstone, and double majoring Computer Science and Psychology at Hunter College- projected to graduate December 2024. Previously, I have served as a Mentee at Bloomberg, a Data Science Intern at the Sadie Nash Leadership Project, and the CEO/Brand Designer for Fiber Femme, a handmade crochet and jewelry business featured in Material Grrrrlz Magazine. In my free time, I love contributing to the queer community by managing Go! Magazine's Pride Month operations and web designing for Queer Athena, an event planning LLC.
